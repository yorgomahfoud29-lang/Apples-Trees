Biography

Hi, I’m Leana Khoury. I was born in Lebanon and grew up in a small town by the beach.

My memoir started as both a personal challenge and a way to grow: I wanted to push my writing further, but also find a place to release and understand my thoughts. What began as an achievement slowly turned into something more honest—a space where I could confront my experiences and give them meaning.

I’ve always been interested in growth, both personal and intellectual, and in understanding how people think, feel, and change.

As I move forward, I plan to study international relations while continuing to write and explore new ways of expressing my ideas.
